Sand Creek Farms

Property Overview

Located north of Saint Anthony, Sand Creek Farms offers 1,021 acres of prime irrigated farmland with nine well-maintained pivot irrigation systems ideal for potato and grain production.

Potato Storage Facility

The property includes a well-maintained 120,000 cwt potato cellar with modern temperature and humidity control systems. The storage facility features:

  • Automated climate control system with efficient forced-air ventilation
  • Drive-through design for easy loading/unloading
  • Built in 2004

This storage facility provides significant added value and operational flexibility for the farm operation.

Fertile Soils for Row Crops

The Sand Creek Farms property consists primarily of Rexburg-Ririe complex and Bannock sandy loam soils, which are ideal for potato rotation with grains and other crops. These well-draining soils have consistently produced above-average yields for the region.

Investment Opportunity

Sand Creek Farms provides not only immediate agricultural returns but also long-term appreciation potential as Idaho farmland continues to be recognized for its value and productivity. The diverse water rights portfolio provides security in changing climate conditions. This carefully maintained operation offers turn-key potential for the next owner to continue the farm's history of successful production.
